About Recycle Coin

Recycle Coin (RCYL) is a blockchain-based solution designed to incentivize recycling efforts and promote environmental sustainability. Earn RCYL tokens for verified recycling activities and join us in making a positive impact on the environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize recycling by leveraging blockchain technology. By rewarding users for their recycling efforts, we aim to increase participation and transparency in recycling programs worldwide.

Key Features

  • Earn rewards for recycling
  • Transparent and secure tracking
  • Support for eco-friendly initiatives

Recycle Coin (RCYL) - A unique cryptocurrency designed to incentivize and reward recycling activities.

About

Recycle Coin (RCYL) is a groundbreaking innovation designed to transform the recycling industry by leveraging blockchain technology. RCYL aims to promote environmental sustainability and responsible waste management through a gamified, community-driven platform that rewards users for their recycling efforts with a limited supply of 10 million coins.
